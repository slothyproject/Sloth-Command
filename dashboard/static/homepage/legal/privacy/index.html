Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 21, 2026. This policy explains what Sloth Lee collects, why data is used, and how we protect your community information.

1. What We Collect

  • Discord account basics like user ID, server ID, role ID, and channel ID.
  • Commands, moderation actions, and ticket activity needed to keep your server running.
  • Dashboard session data and security logs that help prevent abuse.

2. Why We Process Data

  • To provide moderation, ticketing, automation, and reporting features.
  • To keep your server secure by detecting raids, spam, and suspicious behavior.
  • To troubleshoot issues and improve reliability for community owners.

3. Legal Basis

  • We process data to deliver the service you request when you add Sloth Lee.
  • We also process limited security and operational data under legitimate interest to protect servers and users.

4. Data Retention

  • Operational logs are kept only as long as they are needed for moderation, auditing, and safety.
  • Inactive or stale records are routinely cleaned up.
  • You can request deletion of eligible data using the contact methods below.

5. Security

  • We apply access controls, audit logging, and secure infrastructure practices.
  • Only authorized systems and staff can access protected service data.
  • No system is perfect, but we continuously monitor and improve safeguards.

6. Cookies And Similar Tech

  • The website may use essential cookies for sign-in flow, session continuity, and security.
  • We do not use invasive tracking just to sell ads.

7. Your Rights

  • You can request access, correction, export, or deletion of personal data where applicable.
  • Server owners can request removal of configuration data when uninstalling service features.
  • We may need to keep minimal records for fraud prevention or legal compliance.

8. Minors

  • Sloth Lee is not intended for children under the minimum age required by local law and Discord policy.
  • If you believe protected minor data was provided in error, contact us for review and removal.

9. Policy Changes

  • We may update this policy when features or legal requirements change.
  • Major updates will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.

10. Contact

  • Dashboard: https://dissident.mastertibbles.co.uk/dashboard
  • Support: open a support ticket in your server or through dashboard support channels.
